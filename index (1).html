💡 Before You Report
1
Preserve evidence — take screenshots of all conversations, transactions, and profiles before they can be deleted.
2
Record transaction refs — note all bank transfer IDs, RRR numbers, or crypto wallet addresses.
3
Don't confront suspect — do not alert the suspect that you are reporting. It may hinder investigation.
4
Change your passwords — immediately secure all compromised accounts and enable two-factor authentication.
📌 What Happens After You Report
1
You receive a reference number via email and SMS immediately.
2
Your case is reviewed within 48 hours and assigned to the appropriate state unit.
3
Investigators may contact you for more information.
4
You can track your case online at any time using your reference number.
